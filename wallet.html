What are NFTs?

NFTs are non-fungible digital assets mostly referred to as ERC721 and ERC1155 that are either unique or limited in quantity. Think of them like you would a famous painting by Picasso or the video game Gamma Attack for the Atari 2600. NFTs are like vintage baseball cards but only in a digital form that is unique, non-fungible and secured using cryptography. Usually, crypto collectibles are represented as real-life objects such as pets or avatars. Some crypto collectibles are computer generated works of art. Each token has variations in specific attributes and there are limits to the number of tokens that can be generated.


Where do I store NFTs?

Almost all Wallets provide an easy way to store and access all your crypto collectibles with peace of mind and security. Crypto collectibles are secured on the blockchain and associated with a public address. Over the last few years, we have seen various versions of crypto collectibles such as CryptoPunks, CryptoKitties, Decentraland, Lordless and more which have become very popular.
